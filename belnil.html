The title nay relt on its
own no longer. You no longer
value my time how I did
yours. Of all the broad
spells I have turned, the
speech is one I shall offer
to forgive. This is your chance.
Your call to change. To restow
my faith. Of many my own
values had been thrown to
a curb, burn the false morals
you held. I am become
morality itself. Do not peril
of my blunder; this is an
issue most complex. -Sal
