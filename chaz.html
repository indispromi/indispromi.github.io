My dearest friend,
I call to you. My
days have grown
eldest. I am
lost of my liberty.
I am lost of my
fire. Before me,
it still burns.
Now here, I feel
a cold burn. The
day is testing
of my ends and
wits. I hold desires
unspeakable.

The fire is back.
Taken hold a new
form. One I must
keep still. One I
was unaware. By
force. Louder than
what I am
capable of. Destroy
the books of him.
Destroy the books
of her. Distribute
my tellings. Fear me.
