A tune, toned for
ears whom lie dead
in a room far from
here. They do not
listen. Silent. Silent.

Do not think;
Remove yourself.
Dissipate into the
bleak surround.
Some to believe, for
you could not do
it again. If you
could. Sait change
the name of your
own. Become who
is in your head best
for. Be a spell of
the now. Be a spelt
to the future. Let
them learn from
you. When enough
is enough, return
to a cryr basen.

Become the best
for youthful yours.
