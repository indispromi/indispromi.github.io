Lights they have
shone to the heavens
for my discovery. They
are mistaken. You
have been misled
by those of hades. I
shall not follow
your light. It does
not permit my mind.
Come, find me at
nigh, handheld beam.
You have placed
your signs, planted
YOUR toys amongst
my peace. I shant
be lured, for the
antenna lie most
visible. Jets of
yours beam down
below with an
inhumane red.
I see the
perishment
you have left
for me, but
you have insisted
me to avoid the
unknown.

Indispromi.
