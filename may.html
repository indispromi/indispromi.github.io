Many clouds I.
Bliss or immo?


Fate lies below. Those who
dare tremble. Those who fear
lie. A stream. You have disturbed
my work. No longer walking,
I am unable to touch. Of your
souls, many damned, my own
will not be discovered. Pull me
in, and I will kill you astell.

To be. Of. Tobe. Tobof.
An alpralgamation. A zolamigda. You
are one that carries none.
Empty.


A terrible abode. We are above.
Sold atone, atuned, they can
not hurt me, but they can hurt
you.

Indispromi.
