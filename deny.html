Why has it, must it,
all come down to this?

It will be of 28th.
I will kill two of
my own creation. I
will attempt to create
such a scene similar
for my own, but I
will fail greatly. A
hard brake.

The slowing down of
mine. Come to a halt,
screeching. The pole in
front. I have changed
much too late. Now,
I am taken away.
The happy cheer of one.
The angered brow of one.
I am to become of
them. They have not been
destroyed.

|YOU TOLD ME, ON CERTA|
|THAT DAY, THAT I|
|WOULD BE FORGIVEN FOR|
|MY SINS. I AM|
|DAMNED.|
