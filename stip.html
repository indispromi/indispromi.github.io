A confliction I beg catch sight of
less, for it drains my being everso.
Dear lord, why must they hurt eachother?
They know it is not their own fault,
right? Lord above, you would not do such
a thing. You would not! Imposing of bills
we shant be slading to begin of such!
Why have the hours gone by,
but for I haven't still made
of my realization? Is
this who we are
meant to
become?
