A post made. A warning.
A warning to me, one of
their own. A warning,
to not be with them. A
warning, to veer away
from hell's tempt.

In limbo. In limbo. Here
I sit. Here I sit.

I am of neither belong.
They are both of hatred,
of filth. One of them, oh
how it does call to me.
A chance to be hurt once more.

Indispromi.
