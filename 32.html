I may just
understand, finally.
I may just have
peace waiting
beyond the fog.

My peace, it
lay in distal
until the day
I complete
my own duties.

Care for the
many I have
created. They
are betrayed.
Soon to be
of one whole
and pure.
My tales
shant be
cut short!
Though
they sure
will. I do
deserve such.
