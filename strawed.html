Branches of science,
so many I held.
A spirit halted by
none, perhaps I will weld.

A day o'dawn, proud
as ever I. End
of my stairs did
greet a male. Held
a packet in one,
palm shown by one.

I place my fund,
given by my beloved.
Once for the aid,
now become a need.

A tablet I place
unto mine tongue.
Dissolving, my mind
did go with. Slipt
away did my
dreams. A goal
had been set by
mine mind: Relive
the day; rediscover
the tab. Dreamn't.
