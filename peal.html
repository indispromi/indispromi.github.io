A boy I did meet,
there in those
woods one day.
A boy pure. A boy
free of strife.
He shown me his
grateful wooden
sword, full of life.
Oh how life did
love such a boy,
and life he loved
right back.

Alas, was time
for the boy to
go. He wander
back, mighty sword
held proudly above.

One day, I became
one much too
curious. Find that
boy in a forum,
his parents there
they mourn. A poor
boy has died. He
was lost to suicide.
