A lost vision.
Yachts which lie
crest in view.
Few months ago,
oh how big such a
dream. How grand
such a mark. Dreams
of which I hold
no longer. Despite
my genocide, the
wish still does
hold. Fate which
I am spendel
one of. Theirs of
reason I refuse
to hold so dear
to me. Antlers atop
his head, he stare
me down in
moon's light. A
terror, for I
do not know of
what he has
taken. Ingested.
Grand fire burns
for the past of
mine. Hold it no longer.
