Taken aback,
knee to ground,
but one not. Rain
from below. The
lord cries my name
now. Go tell your
staples that I
am no longer a
peaceful creation.
Of how many an
option doth held,
why must I take
such a form? I
shall show you. I
shall end your love
for my being.
Come forth. Hell
swallows me whole
by dawn's break.
Blame forthcast.
Punish theirs of
inno bells. Those
undeserving, let
them take my
hell. I have been
placed unto this earth for
reasons unknown.
