By god, what is yours
of problematic awakenings?
Fuck you for putting
indivine images into my
skull. I shall strip
you down and skin
you alive.

All figures I used to
see now lie dead in
sand. These pyrimial
notes cut into my
melting passion
for an art I
will kill. Ensure
its departure. Only
one section loop
in mine head.
Take One:
All my love was
there in dream.
Was it a beau?
Or was it terr?

torch. I know
one of these
metal bearers.
