Would you truthfully
hold such merits due
forth? A payment you
haveth made! This is
a request most dire.
That is a mistake
most human. Dear, why
must you incriminate
yourself so? Doliet,
a second was not to
be created. Doliet,
you had done so
anyway. Now you are
of a fleed creature.
You are a mau in ahl
terrors. Sleep. Do not rise.

Indispromi.
