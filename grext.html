Librarian stand hunched.
Grasp in palm a
riddle intras. She held
sight. The riddle tore,
burrowed. Spoke to
her in the nigh. What
is the answer? Who is
the figure? A riddle
she clutches. Rows and
columns she searches.
Book by book. Page
by page. None hold
her resolve. A lost
direction. A derailed
life. Oh how her riddle
does breathe.

Night fall. A story soon
to end. She does climb
up the spire. Climb to
the top of her library
roof. There she did
stand, note in hand.
Jumping. Falling. Unto
the depths below.
"I am the answer."
Her lungs pull in. Impact.
